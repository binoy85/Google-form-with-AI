We are a dedicated team of professionals who brought QuizQueue to life. Each member plays a crucial role in the development and success of our project. Learn more about us below:
Binoy Saha
I led the design and development of QuizQueue, focusing on creating a user-friendly experience through iterative prototypes. Initially, I integrated the Google Forms API to enable manual input of questions via a command prompt. Enhancing functionality, I incorporated Google Gemini to auto-generate questions while maintaining the simplicity of the command line interface. Using Figma, I then crafted detailed designs to ensure intuitive navigation and visual appeal. Finally, I coded a responsive web interface, transforming the designs into a seamless, interactive application. My goal was to make QuizQueue as easy and accessible as possible for users at every step.
